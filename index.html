What is UltCast

An Alt-Cast is an alternative broadcast — fan-led audio commentary that runs alongside the live game. Think of it like a group chat with your most passionate friends, but anyone can tune in.

Top-performing Alt-Casts attract tens of thousands of viewers. Until now, that space has been reserved for celebrities and networks. UltCast opens it to everyone.

How It Works

Simple for fans.
Powerful for creators.
